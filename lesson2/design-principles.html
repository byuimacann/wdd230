Visual Hierarchy

Nothing Bundt Cakes

This screenshot portrays the design element of visual hierarchy well. The cake draws the eye first with all of it fun bright colors. The hat on the cake points up leading my eye to the large heading right above it ("Bundt Cakes and More!"). Then my eye goes down to the the page to the colored text area where I then look next at the bolder, all uppercase "Birthday" heading and then continues down the image to the white "Shop Birthdays" button. The use of color, and different font size and weight contribute to the visual hierarchy of this page.
