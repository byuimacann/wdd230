Description of Target Audience

The target audience consists of new local small business owners who range in age from 30-50 years of age, are married with children, enjoy talking with people, and volunteer in their communities.

Persona One

  • Name: Bridger Markham
  • Age:34
  • Gender: Male
  • Occupation: Restaurant Owner

Bridger loves food and enjoys cooking. His wife and four kids are eager to test out any knew recipes that he may want to incorporate into the menu at his restaurant. Bridger enjoys baseball and soccer and spends some of his free-time coaching his kids' youth sports team. Bridger would love to see an increase of traffic into his restaurant but would like to keep advertising and marketing costs to a minimum.

Persona 2

  • Name: Dacia Williams
  • Age:45
  • Gender: Female
  • Occupation: Spa Owner

Dacia enjoys traveling with her husband and 3 teenage kids. She eats healthy and works-out at the gym daily. She works hard to maintain her and her family's health. Dacia believes in overall wellness and also enjoys reading, hiking, and playing the guitar. Dacia wants to share her passion of health and wellness with as many people as she can and would love to connect with other like-minded business owners and individuals to help realize this goal.

Scenarios

1. Dacia wants to network with other local business owners. Could a membership with the chamber of commerce help with this?

2. Dacia would love to try a new healthy place to eat from a business listed on this website. What restaurant businesses are mentioned on this website.

3. Bridger would love to get his restaurant listed on the chamber of commerce website to add to his advertising campaign. Bridger is wondering what he has to do to get this to happen.

4. Bridger is looking for a place for his extended family to stay while they are visiting and would love to refer them to a business listed on the chamber of commerce website.

5. Bridger and Dacia have heard good things about the chamber of commerce but don't know how becoming a member would benefit them. They are wondering if it costs money to join, what the membership benefits are, and what it takes to become a member.
